Side projects have a way of piling up. A LIDAR-to-3D-print pipeline here, a
weekend ray tracer there, a pile of Unreal Engine experiments, the odd
nuclear thermal-hydraulics tool. Most of them live in a GitHub repo and then
quietly gather dust.

This site is where they get a proper home — a place to share the **how-tos and
what-nots**: what I built, why, what broke, and what I'd do differently.

Expect short, practical write-ups, the occasional deep rabbit hole, and code
you can actually copy. Poke around the [projects](/projects), and if something's
useful, that's the whole point.
